HKUST Big Data Institute.

My research interests cover the broad area of networking and distributed systems, with a special focus on big data and machine learning systems, cloud computing, and computer networks. I am particularly interested in identifying fundamenal system design and performance optimization issues in large-scale cloud systems and searching for solutions that are generally applicable, efficient, and easily implementable.

To Perspective Students, Postdocs, and Research Assistants

I am looking for highly motivated graduate students, postdocs, and/or research assistants (RAs) to work on measuring, analyzing, designing, and implementing large-scale networked systems, including distributed machine learning and data analytics frameworks, cluster caching systems, cloud datacenters, and wide-area network. Students with strong hands-on system building skills and/or mathematical background are especially welcomed. Stipend is highly competitive and will be commensurate with qualifications and experience.

If you are interested in working with me, please feel free to drop me an email along with your CV, transcripts, standard test reports (e.g., TOEFL, GRE, IELTS, etc.), and any other documents that you believe can well demonstrate your research capability and potential.
